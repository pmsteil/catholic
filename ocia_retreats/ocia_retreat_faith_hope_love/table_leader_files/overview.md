# Table Leader Overview

## Prayer for Table Leaders

Before the retreat, pray together as a team:

> Lord Jesus, we place this retreat in Your hands.
> We are not the ones who will change hearts—You are.
> Help us to decrease so that You may increase.
> Give us wisdom, patience, and love.
> Protect this day from distraction and disruption.
> Let every participant encounter Your love.
> Mary, Mother of the Church, pray for us.
> St. John Paul II, pray for us.
> St. Thérèse of Lisieux, pray for us.
> St. Augustine, pray for us.
> St. Ignatius of Loyola, pray for us.
> St. John Henry Newman, pray for us.
> St. John of the Cross, pray for us.
> All holy men and women, pray for us.
> Amen.
