# Author's Note

I spent over 37 years thinking about, praying about, and living out what "God is Love" means. It took me just over a year of working on this book to get it into this edition. And I want to give proper credit where it is due.

First of all, I want to thank our Lord Jesus Christ, who is the source of all love and the answer to the problem of evil. He is the one who revealed to me the depths of His love and the meaning of His sacrifice on the cross — and who started His Church through His apostles, continuing through the Holy Spirit to this day.

I am thankful that the Church has preserved the truth of God's love for over 2,000 years through Sacred Scripture, Sacred Tradition, and the Liturgy.

I am thankful and indebted to the saints, the doctors of the Church, and the faithful who have lived out this truth in their lives and have passed it down through the generations.

And then what an amazing age we live in! We have access to incredible resources and tools that can help us understand and share the truth of God's love.

And so, I am thankful for the incredible AI tools that have exploded in 2025-2026 that assisted me in researching, writing, and editing this book. This book is all based on the Church's teachings and the Holy Spirit's inspiration, but Claude Sonnet 4, 4.5 and 4.6 and OpenAI ChatGPT 4 and 5 helped accelerate the research, learning, writing and editing to ensure faithfulness to those teachings.

This is actually the fourth version of this book that I wrote trying to capture the fullness of truth about God's love and His answer to the problem of evil. "Words are hard" as they say. I used AI to help me do the research and spent hours and hours — sometimes on a single word or concept — diving deep into the Scriptures, the Catechism, the saints, and the Church's 2,000-year tradition, hoping to present it in the most meaningful way possible.

I never would have been able to complete this book without using AI to help me do the research. But fear not, this book was not "written by AI." I was the one who directed every word based on what God has been revealing to me through His Scripture and Church over the years. AI cannot understand the depths and complexities of the human heart, and it cannot know the depths and complexities of God's love. But it certainly helped me see the relationships between the concepts and helped me explain them in a straightforward way.

To God be the glory!
